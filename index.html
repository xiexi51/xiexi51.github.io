Xi Xie

About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Connecticut since Fall 2022, advised by Prof. Caiwen Ding. I obtained my M.S. degree in Geophysics from the Institute of Geophysics, China Earthquake Administration, advised by Prof. Zhongliang Wu.

My research interests include high-performance GPU algorithm design and secure and trustworthy AI/ML systems. I am skilled in C++/CUDA programming and CUDA algorithm design, and I enjoy challenging the performance of official libraries.
